The inaugural NZIS Dan Farani Rugby 7's tournament was a resounding success and the wet weather did not dampen the spirits of the competitors who rose the challenge and played like champions. Wainuiomata High won the top trophy but rugby was the real winner on the day. As the heavens opened up hell was unleashed in a fierce display of super 7's rugby that enthralled the parochial crowd.
